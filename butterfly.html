Butterfly Valve .....

 
   
     
 
 
Anital Engineering Works Wafer Type Butterfly Valve have been developed with extensive application, design and manufacturing expertise. Atul Engineering Works High Performance Slim-Seal, Concentric design Butterfly Valves are reliable, maintenance free shutoff with permanently gas-tight seal with an integrally moulded or replaceable elastomer body liner. Anita Engineering Works Butterfly Body having two nos. flange locating holes for sizes up to 12” and from size 14” and above 04 nos. for easy alignment of valve during installation. Flange locating hole confirm to as per ANSI B 16.5 Class 125 & Class 150 / BS 10 Table “D”, “E”, “F” / PN 6, PN 10, PN 16 or other international standard. Anita Engineering Work High performance butterfly valves are also available with Full-Lugs type body with raised faces and flanged ends connection on request.
Anita Engineering Works Butterfly Valve, Wafer Type, Slim-Seal design, maintenance free shutoff. This butterfly valves excess other conventional valves such as gate valve and globe valves in all aspects, especially in terms of light weight, less number of component, no flange gaskets required and economical operation and maintenance costs.
Anita Engineering Works Slim Seal design Butterfly Valves body seat mechanism have a very low torque, tight shut off with zero leakage, compact actuator selection, and long life smooth easy operation. The slim-seal, center disc design valves are widely available with different combination of body, seat & disc materials to satisfy the need of most industrial application.
 

Valve Operation :
Anita Engineering Works Butterfly Valves are generally supplied with manual hand lever operation up to 10” and above gear unit operated. Anita Engineering Works also offering Butterfly Valves with either Pneumatic or Electrical actuator operated for automation.
