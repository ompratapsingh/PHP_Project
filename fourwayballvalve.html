Four Way Ball Valve .....

 
   
     
 
 
 
Anita Engineering Works offers a tremendous value in multi-port three and four way valves. Three Way / Four Way Ball Valves a Specialty of Anita Engineering Works, a leading manufacturer and supplier of Ball Valve and Actuation. We have a wide range of Three Way & Four Way Ball Valve in Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel, Cast Iron and others metal on special request. Our Three Way Ball Valve & Four Way Ball Valve selection covers Full Port & Regular (Reduce) Port, Flanged End, Screwed End, Socket Weld, Butt Weld (Welded Neck) End & Tri-Clover End with Manual Hand Lever, Gear or Pneumatic Operation.
 

 

Valve Operation :

Anita Engineering Works Ball Valves are generally supplied with manual hand lever or gear unit operated, depending on valve size.Anita Engineering Works also offering Ball Valves with either Pneumatic or Electrical actuator operated for automation.

 

Wide Range of Body Seat, Seal & Packing :
Anita Engineering Works Ball Valves are also available with different type of Seat, Seal & Packing material such as P.T.F.E. (Virgin) / Glass Filled P.T.F.E. / Carbon Filled P.T.F.E. / Graphite (Grafoil) / PEEK to suit special requirement of client.
