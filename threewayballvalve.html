Three Way Ball Valve .....

 
   
     
 
 
 
Three Way Ball Valve come with either “L” Port or “T” Port for suitable service condition. Mixing and Diverting Type Three Way Ball Valves are also available. Four Way Ball Valve available with Double “L” Port. The Three Way & Four Way Ball Valve features four seats for equal seat loading. Multiple Gland Packing Seal & Spring Washer gives zero leakage from gland portion.
 
Three Way Ball Valve (Flanged END) Three Way Ball Valve (Screwed END)
 
 

Anita Engineering Works Covers Size range for Multiport Ball Valve in Screwed / Socket / Butt Weld End from ½” to 2” and in Flanged End from ½” to 6”, Pressure Rating up to 300#

Anita Engineering Works P.T.F.E. seated Ball Valves gives Zero Leakage, Tight Shut-Off and Low Torque with easy smooth operation at temperature ranging from -200 C. to +2200 C. Electro-Polished, Buff Finish & Ultra clean valve also provided for Food & Pharmaceutical Industries on request.

 
Valve Operation :

Anita Engineering Works Ball Valves are generally supplied with manual hand lever or gear unit operated, depending on valve size. Anita Engineering Works also offering Ball Valves with either Pneumatic or Electrical actuator operated for automation.
 
Wide Range of Body Seat, Seal & Packing :

Anita Engineering Works Ball Valves are also available with different type of Seat, Seal & Packing material such as P.T.F.E. (Virgin) / Glass Filled P.T.F.E. / Carbon Filled P.T.F.E. / Graphite (Grafoil) / PEEK to suit special requirement of client.
